Kathlynn Lui

I'm a UX designer and web developer from Adelaide.

I build websites on WordPress and sometimes build user interfaces on Ruby on Rails.

Experienced in HTML5, CSS3, Sass, JavaScript (including the jQuery library), PHP (WordPress mainly) and Responsive Web Design. I've had experience with front-end frameworks like Bootstrap. I have also dabbled in ReactJS, & ASP.NET and enjoy learning about new technologies.

A self-taught designer. I always find myself being attracted to design and typography and I'm attempting to become better at design in general. Sketch and Photoshop are my favourite design tools.

My passion is to make unique websites that set themselves apart from standard websites. Love to solve problems with strategy. Always excited to do something new and something different every single day. This is what drives me, this is why I do what I do and enjoying every moment of it.
