About Me

I graduated from the University of Adelaide in 2011 with a Bachelor of Computer Science (Software Engineering). It wasn't until my final year that I found out that I have developed a passion for the web, which led me to obtain a Diploma in Website Development at TAFE South Australia.

My focus does not only lie solely on building websites, I am very involved in the entire web development process as well; right from the planning phase to launching and training. I love being part of the discovery workshop, where I can experience first-hand, the clients' pain points and brainstorm a solution to solve their problems.

Through my experience, I came to realise that a website is more than just an online presence. Clients would come to me seeking advice on how to generate more visitors and sales on their websites. That is when I decided to upskill and enrol into a digital marketing course. I believe with this knowledge, I am able to add more values to the websites that I create.
